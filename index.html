About Us

We are a bar and restaurant that serves a variety of food - Indian, Western, and Asian. We also serve beer and alcohol.

We are located at Jalan Tun Sambanthan, Brickfields right across KL Sentral. Come to the Guru to chill with friends and family, anytime between 12 noon to 12 midnight!
